Short description

A novel macrophage platform for solid tumors

We are developing a ready-to-use allogeneic macrophage cell therapy for cancer: first-in-class, broad tumor specificity as it is antigen-independent, unique resistance to exhaustion within the tumor, and industrially scalable manufacturing.

A novel macrophage platform translates scientific breakthrough into investable value and eliminates key bottlenecks in today’s cell therapies for solid tumors.

The problem

  • Cancer is the second leading cause of death worldwide.
  • Existing cell therapies are expensive and largely ineffective against solid tumors.
  • T-cells are antigen-dependent and rarely penetrate solid tumors.
  • Immune cells become exhausted within the tumor.
  • Macrophages are unable to proliferate.

Solution

New xMACx macrophage cell therapy attacks solid tumors through a completely novel biological approach: metabolic enhancement, high proliferative capacity, resistance to exhaustion and persistent, stable anti-tumor effect at low cost.
